1. What is Square Eyes?

SquareEyes is a movie streaming platform dedicated to showcasing independent films and supporting small filmmakers. We offer a diverse selection of indie films and shorts from around the world.

2. What types of films are available on SquareEyes?

We offer a wide range of films, including independent dramas, comedies, action, shorts, and experimental films. Our library is constantly updated with new and exciting content from emerging filmmakers.

3. Can I watch SquareEyes on multiple devices?

Yes, you can watch on multiple devices, including smartphones and computers.

4. How can I support my favorite filmmakers on SquareEyes?

By purchasing and watching their films and spreading the word, you help promote independent filmmakers. You can also share their work on social media and follow them for updates on their latest projects.

5. I am a filmmaker. How can I submit my film to SquareEyes?

We’re always looking for new and exciting content! Get in touch with us on submit@squareEyes.com. We look forward to seeing your work!

6. What should I do if I experience technical issues?

If you encounter any technical problems, please contact our support team at support@squareEyes.com or call us at +1 (123) 456-7890 during business hours.
